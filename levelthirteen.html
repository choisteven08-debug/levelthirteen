// About

Built by students,
for students.

What began as a small student-led club has grown into a broader platform. We started with mentorship and career exploration, and have since expanded to cover a wide range of post-secondary pathways — from business to STEM, arts, and beyond.

As we grow, we remain dedicated to providing students with the resources and guidance they need to make informed decisions about their futures — at every stage of high school, not just Grade 12.

Student-Run Non-Profit Organization

Level Thirteen envisions a movement that redefines how students access post-secondary education. By connecting aspiring students with experienced mentors, demystifying academic and career pathways, and showcasing opportunities to excel, we are building an inclusive community that transcends individual schools and geographic boundaries — where knowledge is shared freely, potential is universally recognized, and ambitious students have the resources to chart their own path to success.

// The Problem

Is There a Need?

Too many students hit Grade 12 without a plan. That's when the stress, bad decisions, and missed opportunities kick in. Level Thirteen helps you start now — so you'll be confident, prepared, and already ahead of the game when it's time to apply for post-secondary.

// The Solution

What We're Building

  • 01Connect you directly with top students across the country, no matter your area of interest.
  • 02Break down what each academic pathway actually looks like — STEM, arts, health sciences, and beyond.
  • 03Spotlight key opportunities, competitions, and programs to help you stand out.
